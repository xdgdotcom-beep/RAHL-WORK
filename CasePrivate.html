02 — Signature Molecular Techniques
02
Spherification
Sodium alginate & calcium chloride transform liquid into perfect orbs — a cocktail-in-a-sphere that bursts on the palate.
Liquid Nitrogen
Flash-freezing at −196°C creates instant ice creams, frozen garnishes and dramatic theatrical fog that rolls over the bar.
Smoke & Flame
Smoking guns infuse wood, herb or spice aromas. Open flame caramelises sugars and scorches citrus for complex layered profiles.
