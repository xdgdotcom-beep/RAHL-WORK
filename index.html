Meet Rahul AK

Fashion is not just clothing — it is the language of identity, culture, and artistry made visible to the entire world.

Rahul AK is a celebrated fashion designer and educator with over 8 years of hands-on experience spanning haute couture, ready-to-wear, sustainable fashion, and digital design. Having collaborated with leading brands across Mumbai, Delhi, and international fashion weeks, Rahul brings real-world industry knowledge directly into the classroom.

His mission at XD Enterprises is simple: to make world-class fashion education completely free and accessible to every aspiring designer — regardless of background or financial situation. He believes that talent, not tuition fees, should determine who becomes the next great fashion creator.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know about our completely free fashion education at XD Enterprises.

Are all courses truly 100% free?
+
Yes — every course is completely free. No credit card, no hidden fees, no freemium traps. XD Enterprises and Rahul AK believe talent, not finances, should determine who becomes a great fashion designer. Enroll in any or all 12 courses at zero cost, with lifetime access included forever.
Do I need prior design experience?
+
No prior experience is necessary for beginner courses. All you need is passion and willingness to learn. For intermediate and advanced courses, the relevant beginner knowledge helps, but we provide supplemental resources so nobody gets left behind.
Are the courses live or pre-recorded?
+
All courses feature pre-recorded HD video lessons accessible 24/7 at your own pace. Additionally, Rahul AK hosts scheduled live Q&A sessions open to all enrolled students — these are also recorded for you to revisit at any time.
Will I get a certificate after completing?
+
Yes! Every course comes with a free digital certificate of completion issued by XD Enterprises and signed by Rahul AK. The Complete Masterclass awards an official XD Fashion Design Diploma, recognised by partner brands and industry recruiters across India.
How long is course access valid?
+
Lifetime access — completely free. Once enrolled, there are no expiry dates or renewal fees. Study at your own pace, revisit lessons as many times as needed, and receive any future course updates automatically at no charge, ever.
Can I enroll in multiple courses at once?
+
Absolutely — since all courses are free, enroll in as many as you like simultaneously. Many students start with Fashion Design Fundamentals alongside Fashion Illustration to build both technical and artistic skills from day one.
What materials will I need?
+
Each course includes a detailed materials list with budget-friendly options. Illustration courses need basic art supplies. Construction courses benefit from a sewing machine. Digital courses need a computer — free trial versions of most software are available and we guide you through every option.
What career support does XD Enterprises offer?
+
All students receive portfolio feedback, career guidance sessions, and access to our active graduate community. We maintain relationships with fashion companies across India who regularly connect with our graduates. Masterclass students also get direct introductions to partner brands and studios.
