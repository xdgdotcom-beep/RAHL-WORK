In the modern cocktail scene, the terms “mixology” and “mixologist” are a regular part of bar vocabulary — being used more and more to describe a style of mixing cocktails and the people who practice it. Subhash Sankar exemplifies this distinction at the highest level.

With 19 years of experience spanning India, the UAE, Bahrain, Qatar, Belize, and the Turks & Caicos Islands, Subhash is a Lead Molecular Mixologist with a deep passion for gastronomy-led cocktail design and elevating every guest experience into a multi-sensory performance.
