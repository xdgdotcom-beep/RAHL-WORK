The Artisan

Crafting liquid experiences

In the modern cocktail scene, the terms "mixology" and "mixologist" are a regular part of bar vocabulary — being used more and more to describe a style of mixing cocktails and the people who practice it. Subash Shankar exemplifies this distinction at the highest level.

With 19 years of experience spanning India, the UAE, Bahrain, Qatar, Belize, and the Turks & Caicos Islands, Subash is a Lead Molecular Mixologist and Beverage Program Developer with a deep passion for local ingredients and gastronomy-led cocktail design.

"A cocktail is not just a drink — it is a story told in glass, fire, smoke, and flavour."

— Subash Shankar
