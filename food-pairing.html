A refined Mexican–Peruvian table to complement the cocktail odyssey

The Mexican Peruvian Table

Paired seamlessly with a refined Mexican Peruvian menu, this experience weaves together bold, vibrant tastes and innovative textures, inviting guests to savour every sip and bite in a celebration of artistry and tradition.

01
Appetizers
To open the palate
Ceviche de Camarón y Mango
Shrimp ceviche with mango, Peruvian chili, and toasted corn.
Tlayuda de Pulpo
Grilled octopus on crispy Oaxacan tlayuda with black olive salsa.
Anticuchos de Corazón
Beef heart skewers marinated in Peruvian spices, served with ají verde.
Tamal de Pato
Duck tamal with mole negro and pickled vegetables.
Chicharrón de Pescado
Crispy fried fish bites with salsa criolla and lime aioli.
02
Main Courses
The heart of the table
Lomo Saltado con Mole Verde
Peruvian stir-fried beef with Mexican mole verde, served with jasmine rice.
Pescado a la Talla
Grilled fish marinated in adobo, served with Peruvian purple potato purée.
Cochinita Pibil con Quinoa
Slow-roasted pork with achiote, served on a bed of quinoa salad.
Arroz con Mariscos y Ají Amarillo
Seafood rice infused with Peruvian yellow chili and Mexican epazote.
Pollo a la Brasa con Salsa de Chile Pasilla
Peruvian rotisserie chicken with smoky pasilla chili sauce.
03
Desserts
A sweet farewell
Suspiro Limeño con Cajeta
Traditional Peruvian sweet cream with Mexican caramel sauce.
Helado de Maíz Morado y Chile Ancho
Purple corn ice cream with ancho chili caramel.
Churros Rellenos de Lucuma
Crispy churros filled with Peruvian lucuma cream and dusted with cinnamon sugar.

Paired seamlessly with cocktails that challenge, comfort, and captivate — every bite and sip a celebration of two great culinary cultures.
