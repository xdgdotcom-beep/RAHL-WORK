Fuego y Magia

The Journey

Under the masterful touch of El Mago, this mixology odyssey transforms cocktail craft into an enchanting sensory journey. Blending ancient techniques of smoke, flame, and barrel aging with avant-garde molecular mixology, El Mago conjures a lineup of cocktails that are as visually stunning as they are complex in flavour.
